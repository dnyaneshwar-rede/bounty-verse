# Getting Started with Bountyverse

Welcome to Bountyverse! This guide will help you get started with our platform.

## What is Bountyverse?

Bountyverse is a platform that connects developers with exciting bounties and rewards them for their contributions.

## How to Get Started

1. Sign up for an account
2. Browse available bounties
3. Apply for a bounty
4. Submit your work
5. Get rewarded!
